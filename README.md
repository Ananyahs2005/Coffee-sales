# Coffee-sales
1. Project Title
Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze coffee shop sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product trends. The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue, sales patterns, store performance, and product profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

2. Short Description / Purpose
The Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard is a business intelligence solution built using Power BI to help analyze sales data from multiple coffee shop locations.
The dashboard enables users to monitor revenue, identify best-selling products, analyze customer purchasing trends, compare store performance, and uncover business opportunities through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was developed using the following technologies:
   Power BI Desktop – Dashboard creation and interactive visualizations.
   Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
   DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Custom measures, KPIs, and calculated columns.
   Data Modeling – Relationship creation between tables for efficient filtering and analysis.
   File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard screenshots.

4. Data Source
Dataset: Coffee Shop Sales Dataset(Kaggle)
The dataset contains transactional records of coffee shop sales including:
Order ID
Order Date
Order Time
Product Category
Product Type
Product Detail
Store Location
Quantity Sold
Unit Price
Total Amount
The data represents customer purchases across multiple store locations and enables sales trend analysis over time.

5. Business Problem
Coffee shop managers need a centralized dashboard to monitor sales performance, customer demand, and product popularity.
Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to:
* Identify top-selling products.
* Monitor daily and monthly revenue.
* Compare performance across store locations.
* Understand customer purchasing patterns.
* Optimize inventory and staffing.
This dashboard solves these challenges by providing real-time insights into business performance.

6. Dashboard Features / Highlights
* Sales Overview – Displays key KPIs including Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Quantity Sold, Average Revenue, and Total Products.
* Revenue Analysis – Tracks revenue trends by month, day, and hour to identify sales patterns.
* Product Performance – Analyzes revenue and quantity sold by product category, product type, and product detail.
* Store Performance – Compares revenue and order distribution across different store locations.
* Interactive Filters – Enables dynamic analysis using slicers for month, product category, product type, and store location.
* Business Insights – Includes visualizations such as donut charts, treemaps, bar charts, waterfall charts, and gauges to support data-driven decision-making.

7. Business Insights
* Sales Trends – Monitor revenue patterns across different time periods.
* Product Performance – Identify top-performing and low-performing products.
* Store Comparison – Evaluate sales performance across store locations.
* Customer Behavior – Analyze purchasing patterns by day and hour.
* Business Decisions – Support inventory planning, marketing strategies, and operational improvements through data-driven insights.

8. Skills Demonstrated
   Power BI Dashboard Development, Data Cleaning & Transformation, Data Modeling, DAX Calculations,
   Data Visualization, KPI Development, Sales Analysis, and Business Intelligence.
